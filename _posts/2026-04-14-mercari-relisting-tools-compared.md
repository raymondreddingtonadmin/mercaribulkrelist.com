# Best Mercari Relisting Tools Compared: 2026 Honest Review

If you sell on Mercari, you already know that relisting is one of the most effective ways to boost sales. Fresh listings get more visibility in search results, and sellers who relist regularly sell significantly more than those who don't.

The question is which tool to use. There are several options in 2026, ranging from free Chrome extensions to full-featured crosslisting platforms that cost $50+/month. This guide compares them all honestly — what they actually do well, where they fall short, and which one makes sense depending on your situation.

## The Contenders

### RelistHQ — $15/month (Free: 5/month)

RelistHQ is a Mercari-focused Chrome extension that relists one item at a time. You visit a listing, click the extension, and it copies the details into a new listing form.

**Pros:**
- Built specifically for Mercari (not a cross-platform afterthought)
- Copies all listing fields including photos
- Auto-deactivates the old listing
- Helpful blog content for Mercari sellers

**Cons:**
- Single-item relist only — no bulk selection
- $15/month is expensive for a single-function tool
- Free tier is limited to 5 relists per month
- Lifetime deal at $149 is available but still a significant investment

**Best for:** Sellers with small inventories (under 30 items) who want a simple, reliable single-item relist tool and don't mind the price.

### Product Relister for Mercari — Free (Abandoned)

This was one of the original Mercari relist extensions. It has about 1,000 users, a 4.7-star rating, and was genuinely useful when it was actively maintained.

**Pros:**
- Free with no usage limits
- Simple one-click relist workflow
- Loyal user base

**Cons:**
- Last updated May 2024 — over a year without an update
- Breaks when Mercari updates their site (multiple reviews mention this)
- Missing fields: doesn't always transfer subcategory, brand, or certain attributes
- Still in "beta" after years
- No bulk relist capability
- Developer communication is via email only

**Best for:** Sellers who want a free option and are willing to deal with occasional breakage and incomplete transfers. Not recommended as a primary tool since it's effectively abandoned.

### Vendoo — $8.99-$69.99/month

Vendoo is a full crosslisting platform that supports 10+ marketplaces including Mercari. It includes a relist feature, but relisting is one small part of a much larger platform.

**Pros:**
- Cross-platform support (eBay, Poshmark, Depop, Etsy, and more)
- Inventory management, analytics, and bulk actions
- Mobile app available
- Active development and support

**Cons:**
- The $8.99/month plan only covers 25 items
- Most active sellers need the $29.99-$69.99 plans
- Relist/delist is a $4.99/month add-on on top of your plan
- Mercari relisting is not the primary focus — it's one feature among many
- Overkill if you only sell on Mercari

**Best for:** Multi-platform sellers who need crosslisting across 3+ marketplaces and want a single tool for everything. Not cost-effective if you only sell on Mercari.

### Crosslist — $29.99-$44.99/month

Similar to Vendoo, Crosslist is a crosslisting platform with relist capability. It supports bulk relisting of up to 100 items at once on Mercari.

**Pros:**
- Bulk relist up to 100 items
- Supports 11+ marketplaces
- 4.6 stars on Trustpilot with 700+ reviews
- Delists before relisting automatically

**Cons:**
- Starting price of $29.99/month
- Primarily designed for crosslisting, not Mercari-specific relisting
- Complex interface with more features than most Mercari-only sellers need
- US, UK, Canada, and Australia only

**Best for:** High-volume multi-platform sellers who need both crosslisting and relisting in one tool.

### Mercari Bulk Relist — $4.99/month (Free: 3/day)

Full disclosure: this is our extension. Mercari Bulk Relist is a Chrome extension built specifically for one thing — bulk relisting on Mercari as fast as possible.

**How it works:**
1. Go to your Mercari listings page
2. Checkboxes appear next to each listing
3. Select the items you want to relist
4. Click relist — the extension processes each item automatically
5. Copies everything: photos, title, description, price, brand, category, condition, shipping, smart pricing, color, size
6. Deactivates original listings after successful relist

**Pros:**
- True bulk relist — select and relist multiple items at once
- Copies all fields with high fidelity (no missing attributes)
- $4.99/month for unlimited relisting (cheapest paid option in this list)
- Free tier: 3 relists per day (enough for small closets)
- Human-like delays to avoid velocity detection
- Built specifically for Mercari

**Cons:**
- New extension (launched April 2026) — fewer reviews than established competitors
- Mercari-only (no cross-platform support)
- No scheduling feature yet (planned for a future update)

**Best for:** Mercari sellers of any size who want fast, affordable bulk relisting without paying for cross-platform features they don't need.

## Which One Should You Pick?

**If you only sell on Mercari and have 30+ listings:** Mercari Bulk Relist is the clear choice. It's the only Mercari-specific tool with bulk relist capability, and it's $10/month cheaper than RelistHQ while doing more.

**If you sell on Mercari and 2+ other platforms:** Vendoo or Crosslist makes more sense since you get crosslisting bundled in. But expect to pay $30-70/month.

**If you only relist a few items per week:** Product Relister for Mercari is free and works (when it works). Just be prepared for it to break when Mercari updates their site, with no guarantee of a fix.

**If you want a premium single-item experience:** RelistHQ is polished and reliable, but $15/month for single-item relist is hard to justify when bulk alternatives exist at a third of the price.

The right tool depends on your volume, budget, and how many platforms you sell on. But for pure Mercari relisting at scale, the market now has a dedicated bulk option that didn't exist before — and it's the most affordable paid option available.
